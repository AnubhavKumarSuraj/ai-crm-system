# 💰 BUSINESS MODEL DOCUMENT

---

# 📘 Overview

This document defines the business strategy for the **AI Customer Retention System**.

The product is designed as a **SaaS (Software as a Service)** solution targeting small and medium businesses.

---

# 🎯 Target Market

## Primary Users

* Local shop owners
* Ayurvedic clinics / wellness centers
* Gyms
* Coaching institutes
* Small service providers

---

# 🚨 Problem

Small businesses:

* Lose customers due to lack of follow-up
* Do not maintain customer records
* Do not use digital communication effectively

---

# 💡 Solution

An AI-powered system that:

* Stores customer data
* Automates follow-ups
* Sends personalized messages
* Improves customer retention

---
